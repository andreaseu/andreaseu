IT Consultant

I am a full-time IT consultant with a focus on networks and IT security, Microsoft Server and Cloud products with more than 15 years of professional experience from medium-sized to large companies. On a part-time basis, I work on various projects, including Linux services, Apple and Smarthome solutions.

Have knowledge of network planning and implementation (Aruba, Lancom, Ubiquiti UniFi), HPE hardware, traditional backup solutions (Veeam), configuration of security appliances (Sophos UTM, XG), infrastructure virtualization (VMware), database systems (Oracle, Microsoft), groupware solutions (Exchange), security solutions (Sohos, Symantec) and cloud solutions (Office365, Azure).

I offer professional advice and support and concentrate on a needs-oriented solution for every business case. I also have experience in working and scripting and automation with the PowerShell, Pyhton etc.

I am an accredited network specialist with several Sophos, Ubiquity and Lancom awards.
